##  Historical Time Arenas (Core Innovation)
Users are placed in **real past market scenarios** (e.g., Apple 2012) where they:
- Decide how much to invest
- Choose holding duration
- Select investment strategy

Results are revealed with:
- What actually happened in the market
- Points earned
- Clear learning outcomes

This helps users understand **decision quality**, not luck.
