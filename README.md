#  Stock Heroes

Stock Heroes is a **gamified stock market learning app** that helps beginners and intermediate users learn investing **without risking real money**.

The app combines interactive learning, quizzes, virtual trading, and historical market challenges to build confidence through practice.

---

## Problem Statement
- Stock market platforms are complex and intimidating  
- Beginners fear losing real money  
- No engaging way to practice investment decisions
